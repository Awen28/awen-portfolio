# Awen28 - Premium App & Web Development Agency

## Overview
Awen28 is a specialized software development agency based in Austria, focused on creating high-quality mobile applications (primarily iOS) and websites. Founded and led by Thomas Mayrl, the agency combines technical excellence with creative design to deliver digital products that stand out.

The name "Awen" derives from Welsh mythology, meaning "flowing inspiration" or "poetic inspiration" - the creative force that drives innovation. The number 28 represents abundance and prosperity in numerology, reflecting the agency's commitment to creating successful, wealth-generating digital solutions for clients.

## Primary Focus Areas
- **iOS App Development**: Native Swift development for iPhone and iPad
- **Web Development**: Modern, responsive websites and web applications
- **UI/UX Design**: User-centered design that converts visitors into customers
- **AI Integration**: Leveraging artificial intelligence for innovative features
- **B2B2C Solutions**: Connecting businesses with end customers through digital platforms

### 5. Suremate & Suremate Pro - Insurance Claim Ecosystem
**B2B2C Insurance Damage Management Platform**

A comprehensive two-app ecosystem revolutionizing insurance claim processing in Austria.

**Suremate (Free - End Customer App)**
Universal insurance claim reporting app for all types of damages: car accidents, household damage, personal injury, property damage, and more.
[App Store Link: https://apps.apple.com/at/app/suremate/id6475345859]

**Problem Solved**: Policyholders often don't know how to properly report damages or what information their insurance needs.

**Features**:
- Step-by-step guidance through the insurance claim process
- Documentation of any damage type (Auto, Haushalt, Unfall, etc.) with photos and details
- Structured information collection for damage reports
- Professional support throughout the entire process
- Registration with AgentCode to connect with personal insurance agent
- Reduces stress and uncertainty during difficult situations

**Suremate Pro (Agent Management App)**
Professional tool for insurance agents to manage their customer base and all submitted claims.
[App Store Link: https://apps.apple.com/at/app/suremate-pro/id6502916525]
[Web Portal: https://www.awen28.com/suremate-pro]

**Features**:
- Complete overview of all customers who registered via AgentCode
- Access to all damage reports submitted by customers through the Suremate app
- Real-time notifications of new claims
- Customer database with up-to-date contact information
- Mobile access to all client damage reports without requesting documents again
- Streamlined communication between agent and policyholder

**Business Model**: B2B2C platform connecting insurance agents with their clients, digitizing the traditional claim reporting process.
